Football

I have played football for most of my young life but high school football was the most fun I had playing the sport. I played as an offensive lineman, either guard or tackle but mostly tackle. The bond you build while playing football can never be broken. I will never forget the games I played on the field with my teammates. It will be fun to talk to my children about all the experiences I had playing football as a Walpole Rebel.
