Welcome to Walpole.

Walpole, Massachusetts

Walpole is a town about 40 minutes south of Boston. It is a town that borders Foxborough, Massachusetts where Gillette Stadium houses the New England patriots. Walpole is a very big sports town and has a few famous sports figures to come out of the town.The most famous being Joe morgan, a Red Sox manager, and Todd Collins, a former NFL quarterback. But the best part about Walpole is local pizza and sub restaurant Rico's. It is home to a famous wrap called the BJ wrap, consisting of chicken, bacon, cheese, french fries, and a special sauce.
